## Style Prompt

Premium travel editorial meets prompt-workflow explainer. The video should feel like a collectible tourism poster being turned into a reusable creative recipe. Use warm paper tones, ink-black typography, deep indigo UI labels, and one vermilion accent. Motion should feel polished and calm: poster parallax, soft vertical wipes, readable caption beats, and a confident final product CTA.

## Colors

- Background: `#f5ead8` warm poster paper
- Foreground: `#17120d` near-black editorial ink
- Secondary: `#2f4f5f` muted travel indigo
- Accent: `#c94c2d` vermilion stamp
- Surface: `#fff8ed` cream card

## Typography

- Display: Georgia or Times New Roman, high-contrast editorial serif
- UI/body: Arial or Helvetica, compact and readable
- Use large, short lines. Never place long paragraphs on screen.

## Motion

- 9:16 vertical composition with mobile UI safe margins.
- Use poster-scale image movement, not chaotic cuts.
- Scene transitions use paper/card wipes, not hard jump cuts.
- Captions should enter as concise labels with staggered timing.

## What NOT to Do

- No neon tech palette.
- No busy full-screen gradients.
- No random stock-video feel.
- No tiny prompt text blocks.
- No copyrighted brand logos or claims.
